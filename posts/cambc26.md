# Cambridge 2026
This spring, along with [Kenny Chau](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kenny-chau-13a3a6333/) and [Matthew Huang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/the-matthew-huang/), I competed in the Easter 2026 [Cambridge Battlecode](https://battlecode.cam/) as team *something else* and placed second worldwide!
