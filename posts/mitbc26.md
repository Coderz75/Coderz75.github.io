# MIT battlecode

Competed solo as team "Lorem Ipsum" and managed to qualify for the final tournament at MIT, finishing top 16.
The journey involved several strategy iterations throughout January, and balancing bot development with my first-year midterms at UW. It wasn't perfect, but I learned a ton about game AI and heuristics.

Huge shoutout to the entire Battlecode community for their constructiveness, and another thanks to Teh Devs for making it a great first year!
